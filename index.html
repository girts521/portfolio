About me

My name is Girts Karcevskis, I am coming from a rather small country in Eastern Europe: Latvia. Since high school my goal was to study and work outsde of Latvia. And at 18 years I left my country. It was a long journey but since then I lived in England, Germany, Netherlands and China.
I slowly started showing interest for programming. When I wanted to do something, but I could not find a ready solution, I wrote my first batch scripts. I found myself researching and spending hours on trying to make it work, fully immersed into the process. This is when I understood that I enjoy it and want to try to learn more. I took a "The Web Developer Bootcamp" on Udemy where I learned the basics of html, css, js, NodeJs, Express and MongoDB. This course really sparked my interest and I started hunting for several udemy courses. I finished Linux command line course, and played with Ubuntu for a few months. Then I took ElectronJs, Python, Docker and even an AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate courses. At some point I understood that I want to work in IT. But I needed to focus on something more speciffic. Thats when I set my mind back to web development and took an online Bootcamp from Latvia, where I learned more about js, React and TypeScript. Currently I am also taking one more course on Algorithms and Data Structures.
For the past 2 years I have been spending all my free time on studying and playing around with different technologies. Often also during my working hours...
Thats why now I believe that I am ready for the next step in my journey: start working as a junior web developer!
